28    /**
29    * Guice extension that supports injection and outjection of dynamic services.
30    * <p>
31    * For example, injecting a stock quote service:
32    *
33    * <pre> {@literal @}Inject
34    * StockQuote quote;
35    * ...
36    * bind(StockQuote.class).to(service(StockQuote.class).single());</pre>
37    *
38    * Injecting many stock quote services:
39    *
40    * <pre> {@literal @}Inject
41    * Iterable&lt;StockQuote&gt; quotes;
42    * ...
43    * bind(iterable(StockQuote.class)).to(service(StockQuote.class).multiple());</pre>
44    *
45    * Exporting an implementation as a stock quote service:
46    *
47    * <pre> {@literal @}Inject
48    * // the service can be controlled by the Export handle
49    * Export&lt;StockQuote&gt; exportedQuote;
50    * ...
51    * // the service is exported at injection time
52    * bind(export(StockQuote.class)).to(service(myQuoteImpl).export());</pre>
53    *
54    * Applying a custom filter to find a specific service:
55    *
56    * <pre> service(StockQuote.class).filter(ldap(&quot;(Currency=GBP)&quot;)).single()</pre>
57    *
58    * Applying custom attributes to an exported service:
59    *
60    * <pre> service(myQuoteImpl).attributes(names(&quot;Currency=GBP&quot;)).export()</pre>
61    *
62    * (the ldap and names utility methods are from {@link org.ops4j.peaberry.util})
63    * <p>
64    * You can also decorate services with additional behaviour:
65    *
66    * <pre> service(StockQuote.class).decoratedWith(someDecoratorImpl).single()</pre>
67    *
68    * or ask for them to be injected directly, instead of using a dynamic proxy:
69    *
70    * <pre> service(StockQuote.class).single().direct()</pre>
71    *
72    * similarly, if you don't want to bother with an Export handle when exporting:
73    *
74    * <pre> service(myQuoteImpl).export().direct()</pre>
75    *
76    * See the <a href="http://code.google.com/p/peaberry/wiki/UserGuide"
77    * target="_blank">User Guide</a> for more examples.
78    *
79    * @author mcculls@gmail.com (Stuart McCulloch)
80    */
